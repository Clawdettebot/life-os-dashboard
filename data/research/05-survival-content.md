# Survival/Prep Content Strategy

## Content Angles
1. **Practical Skills** - things people can actually use
2. **Myth Busting** - what works vs what doesn't
3. **Budget Prep** - you don't need thousands of dollars
4. **Urban Survival** - applicable in cities too
5. **Mental Prep** - mindset is half the battle

## Card Ideas (from earlier prompts)
- Fire starting methods
- Foraging 101
- Water purification
- First aid basics
- Knot tying
- Bug-out bag essentials

## Differentiation
- Your voice: conversational, authentic, slightly chaotic
- Real talk about what actually matters
- Not as serious as typical prepper content
- Humor +实用性

## Content Pipeline
- Weekly skill breakdown
- Monthly "complete guide" roundup
- Interactive: ask audience what to learn next
